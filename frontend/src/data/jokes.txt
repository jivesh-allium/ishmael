# Crypto Whale Jokes — Subtitle one-liners for Pequod

# Nautical-crypto crossovers
Thar she blows — 500 ETH into a memecoin.
Captain Ahab would've been a Bitcoin maximalist.
Every whale surfaces eventually — usually on Etherscan.
The sea is deep, but not as deep as this liquidity pool.
Don't chase the white whale; let the white whale come to your limit order.
We don't fear the kraken — we fear the liquidation engine.
Even Ishmael knew: not your keys, not your doubloons.

# Market wisdom
Nothing humbles a whale like a -40% wick at 3am.
Buy the dip, they said. They didn't say which ocean floor.
The only thing more volatile than the sea is a 100x leveraged position.
In crypto, every candle tells a story — mostly horror.
Diamond hands are just barnacles with better marketing.
A whale's portfolio is just vibes, conviction, and an ungodly gas budget.
Somewhere a whale is mass-buying a token you've never heard of. Sleep tight.

We tracked a whale across twelve DEXs. It was us the whole time.
If your transaction needs a megaphone, you might be a whale.
The blockchain remembers what your portfolio tries to forget.
On-chain data never lies — it just ruins your weekend.

# Existential crypto
In the beginning there was the genesis block, and it was good.
They told me Web3 would be decentralized. Then one wallet moved the market.
Moby Dick was the original rug pull — everyone lost everything chasing one whale.
We are all Ishmael, clinging to the wreckage of our last trade.
